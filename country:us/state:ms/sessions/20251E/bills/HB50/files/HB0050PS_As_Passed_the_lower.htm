Title: HB 50 (As Passed the House) - 2025 1st Extraordinary Session Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - As Passed the lower Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: HB 50 (As Passed the House) - 2025 1st Extraordinary Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2025 1st Extraordinary Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Read House Bill 50 (As Passed the House) AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE TREASURER TO TRANSFER A CERTAIN SUM FROM THE CAPITAL EXPENSE FUND TO THE VICTIMS OF HUMAN TRAFFICKING AND COMMERCIAL SEXUAL EXPLOITATION FUND; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  Upon the effective date of this act, the State Treasurer, in conjunction with the State Fiscal Officer, shall transfer the sum of Two Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($2,500,000.00) from the Capital Expense Fund (Fund No. 6499C00000) to the Victims of Human Trafficking and Commercial Sexual Exploitation Fund (Fund No. 3307800000) created in Section 97-3-54.11, Mississippi Code of 1972. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage. ================================================================================ Raw Text: HB 50 (As Passed the House) - 2025 1st Extraordinary Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2025 1st Extraordinary Session To: Appropriations A By: Representative Read House Bill 50 (As Passed the House) AN ACT TO DIRECT THE STATE TREASURER TO TRANSFER A CERTAIN SUM FROM THE CAPITAL EXPENSE FUND TO THE VICTIMS OF HUMAN TRAFFICKING AND COMMERCIAL SEXUAL EXPLOITATION FUND; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.
